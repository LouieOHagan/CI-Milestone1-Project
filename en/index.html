About Us

We are a friendly group of people who all share the common love for dogs. What started as a group of friends 3 years ago, going for a walk with our dogs once a week turned in to something bigger and better. Every walk we seemed to meet someone new simply by our dogs meeting, which sparked a conversation between us, their owners. Each week we picked up someone new, we met, we walked and we talked, simple right ? As our group got bigger we decided to create a Facebook group and we have been growing ever since!

What We Do ?

Our Furry Friends is operational in 3 countries across Europe, Ireland, England and Sweden but we are always looking to expand. We aim to bring dog owners together online and transform that online relationship in to a new friendship. The beauty of it all is that when you're out making new life long friends, your dog is too! For most of us its just a great way of getting out, meeting new people, walking and talking! So why not try it out ? ITS FREE!
