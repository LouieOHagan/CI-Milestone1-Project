Why Walk With Us

For some, walking your dog can be a long and lonely process for not only us but for our Furry Friend too! Some of us need that buddy or even buddies to accompany us on our adventure. This is where we can help!

We organise 3 walks a week, you can come to none of them, one of them or all of them...thats entirely up to you! You'll always have a place in our heart and are always welcome back the next week! There is no sign up fee, no commitment...nothing, just be there and Walk With Us!

Going to be a few minutes late ? No problem! Just let us know on Facebook so we don't leave without you!
